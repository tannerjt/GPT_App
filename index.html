Getting Started

Select Draw Treatment Poly and draw on the map (within he study area). The polygon represents a fire treatment area and can be submitted with a double click of your mouse. The GPT (Geoprioritization App) will then analyze the area and determine a score based on many different variables. The final score can help compare different proposals to determine which is the highest priorty. This application is just a planning utility and should be used with other forms of analysis.
